About Us

Have you ever thought while going to the barber shop in your locality and then on reaching your shop found that you end  up wasting to much time, but remember that there lack in connectivity in a huge section of society we at ADEN envision to make a social media platform cum employement center where we connect lower income source people to US the users who dont want to waste single moment and get there work done as quick and efficient as possible. We at ADEN vision to save user time wasted in searching by providing them a single platform where they find there day to day work done in place , we help low source income people by expanding their customers throughout their locality .

TO HELP TO STRIVE! TO GREATER HEIGHTS!
